Making things Speedy

Loading data directly from Google can be slow! When you're ready to take your website live:

  • Visit the Data tab of your Cloudstitch project to place your spreadsheet into Production Mode, then
  • Visit the Publish tab to take a snapshot of the data to serve instead of Google's copy.
